Delete your DA ServePro account – quick guide

Quick path: Open appSettingPersonal informationDelete accountConfirm

Finish in 4 steps

Estimated time: ~1–2 minutes
1

Open DA ServePro

Make sure you are signed in with the account you want to delete.

2

Go to Setting

From the menu/gear icon, open Setting.

3

Choose Personal information → Delete account

Inside Personal information, scroll and tap Delete account.

4

Confirm Confirm

Read the warning, enter any required confirmation info, then tap Confirm.

Important:
  • This action is irreversible. Your account and related data will be removed per DA ServePro policy.
  • Back up anything you need (invoices, history, etc.) before confirming.
  • If you use Google/Apple sign-in, deleting this account does not delete data on those services.
Frequently asked questions
Will my subscriptions be canceled?

Account deletion does not automatically cancel third‑party subscriptions. Please cancel them in your store or payment provider.

Can I recover the account later?

No. Deletion is permanent. You may create a new account with the same email if available.

I can’t find Delete account.

Update the app to the latest version and check: Setting → Personal information → Delete account.

Tip: keep your device online during the process.
